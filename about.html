Our Story

Crafting Beauty with Tradition

Mehandi Touch was founded with a passion for creating beautiful and meaningful mehandi designs. Our journey began in Kolhapur, where we discovered the rich cultural heritage of mehandi art and its significance in Indian traditions.

Over the years, we have grown from a small studio to becoming one of the most trusted names in mehandi artistry in Kolhapur. Our commitment to excellence and attention to detail has helped us create countless beautiful memories for our clients.
